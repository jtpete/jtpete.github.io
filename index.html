Faith

At the young age of sixteen I began to follow Jesus Christ's way for my life, instead of trying to do life my way. Now for the past twenty-four years of my life I have pursued what it means continually change my ways to align with His ways. These changes led me to attend Seminary and earn my Masters in Divinity. I lived in Florida, Iowa, and now back in Wisconsin. I have served at small churches, medium churches and large churches. I have invested into the lives of others to help them as they try and navigate life's diffculties.
