A Message from Dr.Thomas Raimann

It is with mixed feelings that I announce my decision to retire from private practice beginning June 1, 2017. I have decided to spend more time guiding future dentists at Marquette University School of Dentistry and helping my parents, Ed and Ted. I would like to thank you for your trust and confidence in letting me serve as your dentist. Some of you have been with me over thirty years, going back to when my mom Ted was my only employee.

Choosing a dentist to succeed me was a very important decision. I wanted to be certain that my patients continue to receive quality dental care, so I have selected Dr. Katie Satula (formerly Gast) to carry on my practice. A graduate of Marquette University School of Dentistry, she is a skilled, caring dentist with the qualifications to continue the practice in a highly professional manner. Originally from Muskego, Dr. Satula has been practicing in Greenfield, and she will tell you more about herself in a subsequent letter.

My incredible staff of Amy, Kristi, MaryJo, Beth, and Angie will stay on and be there with Dr. Satula to continue to provide you with excellent dental care. Your dental records will continue to be maintained at the office. If you would like to have your records transferred, please contact the office at (414) 425-1510.

I invite you to an open house on Saturday, June 24, 2017 from 10am-1pm to introduce you to Dr. Satula and say farewell.

Thank you again for your loyalty, confidence and friendship,
